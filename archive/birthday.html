Happy Birthday to [SAND]!

On July 21st we will turn 2 years old! In true SANDie fashion, we will be out in Dry Top to have a celebratory run with our cherished drinking game. We also have events scheduled for Dry Top achievements and map completion help!
Come join us in celebration for prizes, puns, fun, and of course, Geodes!
Please refer to our schedule below. Keep in mind that the times and activities are subject to change.

Schedule of Birthday Activities

Friday July 21st

Achievement Run 6 pm - 8 pm PST

This will include portals to the Jumping Puzzle, an assortment of achievements for Dry Top, and a Lost Coin completion train.

Saturday July 22nd

Birthday Dry Top Run 12 - 2 pm PST & 6 - 9 pm PST

Both times will include an open Discord, along with trivia and random raffle prizes.
The evening run will include the SAND Drinking Game, whereas the afternoon run does not.

Sunday July 23rd

Dry Top Map Completion 3 pm PST

Sunday July 23rd

[SAND] Guild Picture 6 pm PST*
