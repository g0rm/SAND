[SAND] Fashion Contest

It's Fashion Wars time! In honor of our Birthday, we'd like for you to try out your best looks for two categories we've selected! This year the entries must be sent in via Google Forms below. This will allow everyone more freedom to pose their characters how they want, as well as provide a less chaotic contest compared to doing it live.
Entries will be judged by Muirellthe Moon, Zileve, and Dynamist Tali.
The deadline to submit is July 21st by 11:59 pm PST.
Winners will be announced by July 23rd, Sunday evening (PST times.)
Please take the time to read up on rules, how-to-enter, and additional information below.
This contest is only for [SAND] members.

Category 1: A Tyrian Hero

Who is your character? What makes them a legend?
Show us who your character is in one screenshot. Find the place, pose, and personality that best describes them as a Tyrian Hero worthy of the world they serve!

Category 2: Cosplay!

How devoted are you to your favorite character? What universe do they hail from?
Create a look that is as close to the original as possible! Get creative, and get to cosplaying!

Instructions

  • Please submit a clear, clean picture of the character your GW2 character is cosplaying as alongside your screenshot.
  • You may enter into both categories for two attempts at a prize, but you may only win in one category.

How to Enter

  1. Prepare your character look and pick out your favorite screenshot.
  2. Upload your screenshots to Imgur.
  3. Please fill out the (far) below form and provide the correct information.

Your entries will be posted in an album on the website, so please keep your screenshots reasonably clean and tasteful! Prizes will be announced shortly. Thank you for participating, and Happy Birthday to us all! <3
